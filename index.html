Explore our fragrances

Wunder-Baum air fresheners are made from a carefully selected palette of fragrance ingredients approved by the International Fragrance Association (IFRA), a Switzerland-based official representative body of the fragrance industry worldwide. Its main purpose is to ensure the safety of fragrance materials through a dedicated science program that protects both the consumer and the environment.

Little Trees Europe strives to create and sell a safe product for both consumers and the environment. We partner with leading fragrance companies to develop the perfect fragrances for our products. All fragrances meet or exceed the IFRA and RIFM guidelines as well as our stringent internal safety standards.
